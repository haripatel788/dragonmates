How Dragonmates Works

Sign In Get Started

A step-by-step guide to the main features of the app.

Create an account

Click "Get Started" above and register with your Drexel email address. The system will send a verification link to your Drexel inbox; click it to activate your account. We require a valid Drexel email so that all users are verified students and you can trust the community.

  • Use your @drexel.edu email.
  • If you don’t see the verification email, check spam or wait a few minutes.
  • Only one account per email is allowed; keep your login secure.

Set your preferences

After signing in, visit Roommate Preferences to describe your lifestyle, budget, move-in date, co-op cycle, and other criteria. The questionnaire includes questions about sleep schedule, cleanliness, social habits, and hobbies.

  • Be honest—accurate answers help generate better matches.
  • You can update these anytime if your situation changes.
  • The algorithm uses your answers to compute a match score for each potential roommate.

Browse matches

Head to Browse Roommates and explore profiles ranked by match score. Each card shows a summary of the student’s preferences, hobbies, and estimated compatibility.

  • Click a card to see full details and dealbreakers.
  • Sort by score or filter by specific traits (e.g. budget, year).
  • Save profiles you like or return later; your local settings persist.

Chat with potential roommates

Select a profile you like and start a conversation through the Roommate Chat page. Chat works in real time and only connects you with verified users, so your privacy is protected.

  • Introduce yourself and mention why you think you’d be a good match.
  • Discuss topics like cleaning routines, study habits, or preferred move-in dates.
  • If you decide to room together, exchange contact info or plan a meet-up.

Update your info

As your plans evolve, keep your profile and preferences up to date so matches stay accurate. Add a short bio, switch majors, or adjust your budget if needed.

  • Your profile is visible to other users—it helps them learn about you.
  • Update preferences any time; re‑sorting and scores will refresh automatically.
  • Periodic updates increase the chance of finding a compatible roommate.

Need help?

Explore the site further or reach out via the notifications page if you have questions. Dragonmates is designed to make your roommate search stress-free.

  • Check the notifications bell for system messages or updates.
  • Contact support by emailing support@dragonmates.example (replace with real address).
  • Review the FAQ section if it's available in future releases.
